Bread Porridge

Ingredients

  • bread
  • milk
  • sugar
  • cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Soak the bread in milk. If it's possible, do it overnight.
  2. Put the soaked bread together with milk and cinnamon in a pot.
  3. On medium-high heat, bring to a boil, stirring occasionally so that the porridge doesnâ€™t stick to the bottom.
  4. Reduce the heat and let simmer, stirring regularly, until the bread has broken down and the porridge has become fairly smooth
  5. You can serve with cream, yogurt or any milk of your preference.
